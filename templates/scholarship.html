Su-vishwa Kutumbh Kalyan Yojana - Detailed Information
Tagline:

"Large Families - Strong Values - Empowered Future"

Objectives:
  • To support families with more than three children in education, values, and life skills
  • Families having only three daughters are also eligible for this scheme
  • To connect children from such families with the economic and social mainstream
  • To promote the Joint Family System, moral values, and responsibility
  • To bring Government schemes and private support together on a single platform
Eligibility Criteria:
  • ✅ The family must have a minimum of 4 children (more than 3)
  • ✅ The family must be a permanent resident of India
  • ✅ Age of children: Between 3 years and 25 years
  • ✅ Annual family income (optional cap):
    • Rural: Up to ₹3,00,000
    • Urban: Up to ₹5,00,000
  • ✅ The family must agree to the Terms & Conditions of the scheme
  • ✅ Family Tree / Birth Certificate / Aadhaar verification is mandatory
  • ❌ Families having three or fewer children are not eligible for this scheme
Benefits of the Yojana:
Educational Benefits:
  • School / College guidance
  • Career counseling (Vidyaprem Model)
  • Competitive examination orientation
  • Free or discounted online classes and workshops
Financial Benefits:
  • Scholarship support (Merit-based & Need-based)
  • Books and stationery kits
  • Examination form fee assistance
  • Skill training fee support
Rules & Regulations:
  • Benefits will be provided to the entire family, not to any single individual
  • School / College enrollment of all children is mandatory
  • Discipline, moral values, and social behavior reports of the children will be reviewed
  • Any false information will lead to immediate cancellation of the scheme
  • Benefits will be provided in the form of services/support, not cash
  • Annual re-verification is mandatory
